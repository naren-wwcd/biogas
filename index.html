Biogas Plant

Turning Organic Waste into Renewable Energy

Our Biogas Plants use advanced anaerobic digestion technology to convert biodegradable organic matter — including food waste, agricultural residue, and sewage sludge — into clean, renewable biogas (methane). This gas can be used directly for cooking, heating, electricity generation, or further upgraded into Bio-CNG.

Key Features

  • Capacity: 100 kg/day to 100+ tonnes/day
  • Processes food waste, agri-waste & sewage sludge
  • Produces biogas + organic slurry (bio-fertilizer)
  • Reduces greenhouse gas emissions significantly
  • Modular, scalable design for any site
  • Remote monitoring with IoT-enabled dashboards

Applications

Hotels & restaurants, food processing units, municipalities, hospitals, educational institutions, residential townships, and agricultural farms.

Benefits

Zero waste to landfill · Carbon credit eligible · Energy cost savings up to 60% · Compliance with Solid Waste Management Rules

OWC Machine

Organic Waste Converter — Compost in 24 Hours

Our Organic Waste Converter (OWC) machines are compact, fully automated systems that convert kitchen and organic waste into high-quality compost within 24 hours using aerobic decomposition, heat treatment, and microbial cultures. No odour. No leachate. No landfill.

Key Features

  • Capacity: 25 kg/day to 5,000 kg/day
  • Fully automatic — plug & play installation
  • Processes wet waste, fruit/vegetable peels, cooked food
  • Output: Dry compost (used as organic fertilizer)
  • Volume reduction: up to 90%
  • Low power consumption, stainless steel body

Ideal For

Apartments & gated communities, hotels & resorts, schools & colleges, hospitals, supermarkets, event venues, and municipal wards.

Benefits

Zero wet waste to landfill · OWC-generated compost can be used in gardens or sold · Eligible for BBMP & ULB compliance · Carbon footprint reduction

Bio CNG Plant

Purified Biogas — Clean Fuel for a Greener Tomorrow

Our Bio-CNG (Compressed Bio-Gas) plants upgrade raw biogas to over 95% methane purity, compress it to 200 bar, and dispense it as a drop-in replacement for fossil CNG. It powers vehicles, industrial boilers, and can be injected into city gas distribution networks.

Key Features

  • Biogas purification to 95%+ methane (CH₄)
  • Compression up to 200–250 bar
  • Compatible with CNG vehicles & industrial burners
  • Integrated H₂S & CO₂ scrubbing systems
  • PESO & BIS certified components
  • Capacity: 100 SCMD to 10,000 SCMD

Applications

Municipal solid waste plants, dairy farms, sugarcane distilleries, sewage treatment plants, and agro-industries seeking to monetize waste streams.

Benefits

Eligible for SATAT scheme revenue · Carbon credits under UNFCCC · Reduces diesel/petrol dependence · Higher calorific value than CNG
